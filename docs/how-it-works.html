TweetTip.me

The easiest way to tip and fundraise with crypto


TweetTip.me gives ownership of contract-controlled ether to the proven owners of Twitter accounts.

  1. Create a campagn by submitting a username to Etherem blockchain.
  2. Your campaign is live! Get others to send money to the campaign
  3. Get the receipient to claim by sending a tweet. More info below.

To claim a tip, follow these steps:

  1. Publish a public tweet following this precise pattern "$addressOfContractToClaim,$addressToSendFundsTo" e.g. "0xFdA6118f7496eC899C31839dB12C66aA5e556003,0x627306090abab3a6e1400e9345bc60c78a8bef57"
  2. Trigger claim() method on TweetWallet instance you are claiming, submmitting the status of the tweet above. (e.g. Status will look something like "948272796251914720")
